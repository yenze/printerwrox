Welcome

Sudi Printer Worx, The #1 South Africa's Authorized Printery Dealers and Distributors, was founded and incepted in 2002 as a rainbow - owned Printing supplies and consulting company based in Cape town with the aim of supplying competitively priced, high quality printing equipment to the Southern African industry. Why us

We have served in the Print supplies Industry for the Southern Africa Units since 2002. We are rated amongst the most experienced printing supplies distributor. Our organization’s reputation for excellence, quality, and integrity and on schedule delivery of projects is highly recognized.
